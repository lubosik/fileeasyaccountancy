## Pricing Strategy

### Competitive Positioning
- **Kent Market**: 30-50% below premium competitors
- **Dubai Market**: 20-30% below premium competitors
- **Value Proposition**: Better value with personal service and local expertise

### Service Pricing
- **Bookkeeping**: £50-100/hour (vs £150-300/hour competitors)
- **Payroll**: £40-80/hour (vs £100-200/hour competitors)
- **VAT Returns**: £60-100/hour (vs £100-150/hour competitors)
- **Tax Planning**: £80-120/hour (vs £150-250/hour competitors)
- **Company Formation**: £100-150/hour (vs £200-400/hour competitors)

### Pricing Transparency Strategy
- **Pricing Page**: Clear pricing tiers and packages
- **Service Pages**: Include pricing ranges
- **Consultation**: Free consultation with pricing discussion
- **Value Communication**: Emphasize savings and value

## Form Strategy

### Form Optimization
- **Field Reduction**: Minimize required fields
- **Progressive Profiling**: Collect information over time
- **Smart Defaults**: Pre-fill known information
- **Validation**: Real-time validation and error messages

### Form Types by Page
- **Homepage**: Simple enquiry form (Name, Email, Service, Message)
- **Service Pages**: Service-specific quote forms (Name, Email, Phone, Service, Business Size)
- **Location Pages**: Location consultation forms (Name, Email, Phone, Location, Business Type)
- **Pricing Page**: Pricing inquiry forms (Name, Email, Phone, Package, Budget)

### Form Conversion Optimization
- **Current Average**: 3% conversion rate
- **Target Average**: 5% conversion rate
- **Improvement Strategy**: Reduce fields, improve UX, add trust signals
