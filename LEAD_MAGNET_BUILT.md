## Tool Flow

### Step 1: Form Input
- User enters name, email, and cash flow information
- Form validates required fields (name, email)
- All inputs are optional except name/email

### Step 2: Calculation
- Calculates cash conversion cycle
- Determines daily cash burn rate
- Calculates days until cash runs out
- Identifies trapped cash (retentions + CIS over-withheld)
- Calculates payment term gap and working capital needed
- Determines risk level (Low/Medium/High/Critical)

### Step 3: Results Display
- Shows personalized risk level alert
- Displays key metrics in cards
- Provides actionable steps based on results
- Includes consultation CTA with Calendly link
- Links to Construction Packages page
